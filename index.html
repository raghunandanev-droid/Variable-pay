Understanding Variable Pay

Variable Pay is the performance-linked portion of compensation. Unlike fixed salary, it changes based on company, team/function, and individual performance.

🔢

The Formula — Explained Simply

FINAL VARIABLE PAY =
Target VP × [(Company Factor × CP Weight) + (Div/Fn Factor × DP Weight) + (Individual Multiplier × IP Weight)]

Below 85% company performance, this tool applies zero payout. At 100%, factor is 1.0. Above 100%, bonus multipliers apply and final payout is capped at 125% of target VP.
